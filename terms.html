Terms and Conditions

1. Attendance Policy

a. Students are required to attend all scheduled classes, rehearsals, and lessons.

b. If a student needs to be absent, they must seek permission in advance from their instructor or the administrative staff.

2. Punctuality

If a student is running late, they are allowed a 15-minute grace period. However, any delay beyond 15 minutes will be counted against their attendance and may affect their progress record.

3. Behavior and Conduct

Students are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and courteous manner at all times while on the premises of Music World.

4. Equipment and Facilities

Students are responsible for taking good care of any musical instruments, equipment, or facilities provided by Music World. Any damage due to negligence may result in financial responsibility.

5. Payment and Tuition

a. Students must adhere to the payment schedule and tuition policies set by Music World

b. Failure to pay tuition fees on time may result in the suspension of lessons or other services.
